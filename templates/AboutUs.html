About Us

Our Origin

Welcome to our project on the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the medical field! We are a dedicated team of students passionate about exploring the ways AI is revolutionizing healthcare and improving patient outcomes. Our project aims to shed light on the pivotal role AI plays in modern medicine and its potential for future advancements. We heard about many tragedies and accidents that happened in Hospitals by nurses/doctors who were incapable of solving medical problems of patients. This more than deeply affected us, but it urged us to find a solution.

Our Objectives

We aim to project the role AI could play in the medical field and express the various advantages it would have.
